# Writing Clearly

Consider _Elements of Style_:

- Avoid adverbs. Replace them with stronger verbs.
- Omit needless words.
- Prefer active voice.
- Use concrete nouns. Name the thing, not the category.
- Prefer full sentences. Reserve fragments for lists and headings.
